OUR CONCEPT

These are the main three guiding characteristics behind the design of your home.

Live well - Our build quality is second to none. Unlike other shipping container-based housing construction, our houses are aesthetically pleasing both inside and out and configured to maximize residential comfort and daily function.

Easy to repair- The modular design aspect of our units make them easy to repair and highly suited for use as an income-producing rental property. A damaged interior wall panel or ceiling panel can be removed and replaced within an hour.

Migration-ready - One of the recurring frustrations in modern day living is the necessity of relocating, whether due to changing circumstances relating to employment, family, or for potentially millions ;of Americans in the not too distant future, environmental displacement. Our housing units are designed to be assembled bolt-on (patent pending) with a relatively low level of technical specialization and have been engineered for upgradable use in a wide spectrum of environmental conditions.
